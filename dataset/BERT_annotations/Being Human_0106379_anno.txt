dialog: different periods of time, from pre-history to the
dialog: present day. But there is just one story. Because we
dialog: blend into one developing narrative the lives that we
dialog: show.
text: There is no suggestion of time travel or re-incarnation
dialog: or any other tricksy or mystic device. We can do in real
dialog: time and with real characters what other movies strive to
dialog: do with immortal time travellers and ghosts. Our story
dialog: is thoroughly based in reality and the magic we are
text: dealing with is real, human magic.
dialog: In moving from one character to the next everything will
dialog: change; supporting characters, setting, period. But the
dialog: essential situation of our hero of the moment will relate
text: to the one before and the one following.
dialog: The magic will be in the transitions, in leaving one
text: character and advancing the film through hundreds or
dialog: thousands of years to find our next hero in tantalizingly
dialog: similar human circumstances. The effect of these transi-
dialog: tions across space and time, will let the audience grasp
dialog: the simple truth behind our story; that we are all in it
dialog: together.
dialog: The key to allowing the audience to enjoy this human
text: connection at the heart of the film is that one actor
dialog: will play the six individuals. The feeling will be of
text: the endless, glorious playing and replaying of the simple
dialog: drama of being alive.
